# E-Commerce Profitability Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the profitability of an e-commerce company (BrightCart) by combining order-level transactions, product cost data, and marketing spend data.

The objective is to identify:
- Which product categories are most profitable
- Which sales channels generate the highest profit
- How product returns affect profitability
- Which marketing platforms provide the best return on ad spend (ROAS)

## Tools Used
- SQL
- Excel
- Data Analysis
- Pivot Tables
- Dashboard Visualization

## Dataset
The project uses three datasets:

1. Orders dataset – transaction-level sales data
2. Product catalog – product cost and category information
3. Marketing spend dataset – advertising performance across platforms

## Analysis Performed
- Calculated total revenue, total costs, and profit margin
- Analyzed profitability by product category
- Evaluated performance across sales channels
- Calculated return rates by category
- Analyzed marketing efficiency using ROAS, CPC, and CPA

## Key Insights
- Electronics is the most profitable category with a 31% margin.
- The marketplace channel shows the lowest profitability due to platform fees.
- TikTok Ads and Influencer marketing generate the highest ROAS (>22).
- Email marketing shows the lowest marketing efficiency.

## Business Recommendations
1. Increase marketing investment in TikTok Ads and Influencer campaigns.
2. Focus growth on high-margin categories such as Electronics and Home & Kitchen.
3. Reduce reliance on Marketplace sales channels due to lower margins.
4. Improve product descriptions and packaging to reduce return rates.
